About Me

ROWSTED was born from an obsession that started in a café in Europe — one perfect espresso shot, and I was hooked. That journey led me to becoming an espresso enthusiast and then transforming into a small-batch coffee roaster obsessed with craft, freshness, and flavor.

My goal is simple: to help you discover what fresh, specialty-grade coffee is meant to be. When you open a bag, I want you to inhale deeply, savor the aroma, brew your perfect cup — then come back for more.
