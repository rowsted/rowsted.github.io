Why Only Whole Bean?

Freshness matters. Once coffee is ground, it begins to lose its flavor and aroma within minutes. That rich, complex cup you’re chasing? It starts with grinding right before you brew.

We only sell whole bean coffee to protect the integrity of every roast. Whether you’re pulling espresso, brewing a pour-over, or making a French press, grinding fresh unlocks the full experience.

About Me

ROWSTED started with one unforgettable espresso in a European café — bold, complex, and unlike anything I’d tasted before. I’d been a coffee drinker for years, but that shot sparked an obsession. I dove deep into espresso, chasing the perfect cup. That journey led me to roasting my own beans, and before long, my hobby became ROWSTED — a small-batch roasting company focused on craft, freshness, and flavor without compromise.

MY GOAL: To ruin bad coffee for you — forever. At ROWSTED, every bag is roasted to help you experience what fresh, specialty-grade coffee should taste like. I want you to open it, breathe it in, brew it just right — and fall in love with your cup all over again. Because once you taste the real thing, there’s no going back.
